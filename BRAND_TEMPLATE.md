# ONE82 Brand Template

## Brand Goal
ONE82 should feel modern, fast, and trustworthy for commerce intelligence.
Visual tone is clean, bold, and operational (not playful).

## Primary Logo Asset
- Master logo file: `public/logos/one82-logo-white-centered-v2.png`
- Keep logo centered in layouts unless a layout specifically needs left alignment.
- Do not stretch, skew, rotate, add effects, or recolor the mark.

## Background Rules
- Default background: white.
- Preferred high-contrast usage: dark logo on white.
- Avoid textured backgrounds behind the logo.

## Clear Space & Sizing
- Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to ~12% of the logo canvas width on all sides.
- For square placements (social/profile cards), keep logo visually centered.
- Minimum digital size: 160px width for small placements to preserve legibility.

## Color Direction
- Use the logo’s existing dark ink tone as the primary brand tone.
- Keep surrounding UI neutral (white, light gray, charcoal) to preserve contrast.
- Avoid introducing saturated accent colors near the logo unless required by product UI.

## Typography Direction
- Use clean, geometric sans-serif typography.
- Prefer strong weight for headlines and medium/regular for body text.
- Keep spacing generous and avoid condensed text near the logo.

## Voice & Style
- Confident, precise, practical.
- Focus messaging on intelligence, speed, and clarity.
- Avoid hype language; use outcome-focused statements.

## Approved Usage Checklist
Before publishing any asset, confirm:
1. Correct logo file is used.
2. Logo is centered or intentionally aligned.
3. White/clean background is used.
4. No distortion or recolor is present.
5. Clear space is preserved.
