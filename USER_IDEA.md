MERCHANT STATEMENT AI READER
- Daily/monthly/yearly processing volume toggle based 
- Average ticket size (TXN value)
High average ticket = low TXN fee
Low average ticket = high TXN fee
- Interchange Rates 
Dependent on card brand and how often certain cards are used within that business. High prestige business’s pull in higher level cards, increasing interchange fee and forcing ISO’s to increase their own markup/fee. 
- Card Brand 
Percentage of TXN’s made with higher level cards which come with higher interchange fees 
- Method of processing 
Swiped (low risk ) or hand keyed (high risk)
- Attrition rate 
- Merchant industry (MCC Code) 
Merchant category codes are used to basically determine the risk level of an industry, which helps ISO’s determine prices (high vs low risk) 
- Growth/Shrink patterns
How often credit card volume increases and decreases over time. Fluctuation rate
- TXN type
Volume of debit versus credit TXN’s.
- Profitability per account
Total residual in comparison to cost of support and service provided.
